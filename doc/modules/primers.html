santa_lucia (sequence, primer_concentration, salt_concentration, magnesium_concentration)
Returns the melting temperature of a primer given primer concentration, salt concentration, and magnesium concentration

Parameters:

  • sequence sequence of the primer
  • primer_concentration concentration of the primer
  • salt_concentration concentration of salt in buffer
  • magnesium_concentration concentration of magnesium in buffer

Returns:

    melting temperature

Usage:

    local melting_temperature = primers.santa_lucia("CAGGAAACAGCTATGAC", 0.0000001, 0.350, 0)
